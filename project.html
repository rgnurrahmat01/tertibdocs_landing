    Variation Instruction (VI)

    Yang kami cek: VI number, date issued, description of variation, reference to contract clause, issued by authorized person (Engineer/Architect/Employer's Rep), signed and stamped.

  • ‚úì

    Yang kami cek: Breakdown costs (labour, material, equipment), time extension requested (if any), quotation approved/rejected status

  • ‚úì

    Variation Order (VO)

    Yang kami cek: VO number, reference to VI and VQ, agreed price, agreed time extension, signed by both parties

  • ‚úì

    Valuation of Variation (if VO not agreed)

    Yang kami cek: Valuation method (daywork, measured work, etc.), supporting documents (timesheets, material receipts)

‚è∞ 3. Claims & Notices

  • ‚úì

    Notice of Delay (Early Warning)

    Yang kami cek: Date of notice, event causing delay, reference to contract clause, submitted within time bar (e.g., 14/28 days), delivered to correct party.

    Time bar: Jika notice terlambat, claim bisa otomatis invalid

  • ‚úì

    EOT (Extension of Time) Claim

    Yang kami cek: Claim reference number, event description, delay analysis (as-planned vs as-built), critical path impact, supporting documents (notice, correspondence, photos, weather data), time extension requested, submitted within contractual deadline.

  • ‚úì

    Cost Claim (Prolongation Cost, Loss & Expense)

    Yang kami cek: Cost breakdown (site overhead, equipment idle time, labour, etc.), calculation basis, supporting invoices/receipts, entitlement basis (contract clause reference).

  • ‚úì

    Final Account Claim

    Yang kami cek: Summary of all variations, all approved claims, all deductions, retention release, final balance calculation

Problem Prevention

7 Masalah Konstruksi Paling Sering

Dan bagaimana TertibDocs mencegahnya

1. Time Bar - Notice Terlambat

Masalah: Kontraktor submit EOT claim setelah deadline notice period (misalnya: 28 hari dari kejadian). Claim langsung invalid karena time bar provisions.

Solusi kami: Track semua events yang berpotensi delay. Alert jika mendekati deadline notice. Cek apakah notice sudah disampaikan dengan format yang benar.

2. Variation Tanpa VI Tertulis

Masalah: Kontraktor lakukan extra work based on verbal instruction atau site instruction saja (bukan formal VI). Saat claim payment, ditolak karena "no written Variation Instruction".

Solusi kami: Flag jika ada pekerjaan variasi tanpa VI reference. Verifikasi apakah VI signed by authorized person dan sesuai contract requirement.

3. Programme of Works Tidak Di-Update

Masalah: Kontraktor claim EOT tapi tidak ada updated programme showing delay impact. Engineer reject karena "no demonstrable effect on critical path".

Solusi kami: Check apakah ada baseline programme, updated programme, dan delay analysis (as-planned vs as-built). Flag missing programme documents.

4. Payment Claim Tidak Match dengan BOQ

Masalah: Payment application claim 80% completion untuk item tertentu, tapi measured quantity atau photos tidak mendukung. Consultant reduce certified amount atau tahan payment.

Solusi kami: Cross-check payment claim vs BOQ items, measurement sheets, dan progress photos. Verifikasi konsistensi % completion.

5. Missing Contemporaneous Records

Masalah: Claim EOT for delay tapi tidak ada daily records, site diary, weather log, atau photos dari kejadian. Difficult to prove cause and effect.

Solusi kami: Check availability of supporting documents: site diary, weather data, correspondence, photos with dates. Flag if claim lacks contemporaneous evidence.

6. Cost Claim Tanpa Proper Substantiation

Masalah: Kontraktor claim prolongation cost tapi hanya kasih lump sum figure tanpa breakdown atau supporting receipts. Claim ditolak atau heavily reduced.

Solusi kami: Verifikasi apakah cost claim punya breakdown detail (labour, equipment, materials, overhead), supporting invoices, dan calculation method yang reasonable.

7. Retention & Bank Guarantee Tidak Clear

Masalah: Kontraktor tidak tahu berapa retention yang sudah di-hold, kapan release 50% at practical completion, dan apakah bisa substitute dengan bank guarantee. Final payment jadi lama.

Solusi kami: Track retention percentage per payment certificate. Alert jika mencapai threshold untuk bank guarantee substitution atau practical completion release.
